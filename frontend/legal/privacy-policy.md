# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2025

## Overview

MagicAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Magic: The Gathering deck analysis service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Email address, display name, profile picture (when using Google Sign-In)
- **Authentication Data:** Encrypted passwords, OAuth tokens for third-party sign-ins
- **Subscription Information:** Payment details processed through Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)

### Deck and Usage Data
- **Deck Information:** Decklists, deck names, formats, and metadata you create or upload
- **Analysis Data:** Results from our deck analysis algorithms
- **Usage Analytics:** How you interact with our service (pages visited, features used, time spent)

### Technical Information
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, IP address, device identifiers
- **Cookies and Local Storage:** Preferences, session data, authentication tokens

## How We Use Your Information

### Service Provision
- Provide deck analysis and recommendations
- Store and sync your decks across devices
- Authenticate your account and maintain security
- Process payments and manage subscriptions

### Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our algorithms
- Debug technical issues and optimize performance
- Develop new features based on user behavior

### Communications
- Send service-related notifications
- Provide customer support
- Send promotional emails (with consent, opt-out available)

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data
Your personal information is never sold to third parties.

### Limited Sharing
- **Public Decks:** Decks you mark as "public" are visible to other users
- **Service Providers:** Trusted partners like Firebase (Google), Stripe for payment processing
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights

### Data Security
- All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption
- Passwords are hashed and salted
- Regular security audits and updates
- Firebase's enterprise-grade security infrastructure

## Your Rights and Choices

### Account Control
- **Access:** View all your personal data
- **Update:** Modify your profile and preferences
- **Delete:** Request account deletion (data removed within 30 days)
- **Export:** Download your deck data in standard formats

### Privacy Settings
- **Deck Visibility:** Choose public or private for each deck
- **Email Preferences:** Opt out of promotional communications
- **Analytics:** Opt out of non-essential analytics tracking

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Data deleted after 24 months of inactivity
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data removed within 30 days of deletion request

## Third-Party Services

### Firebase (Google Cloud)
- Handles authentication and data storage
- Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
- Data stored in secure, compliant data centers

### Stripe
- Processes payments securely
- We do not store credit card information
- Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy

### Scryfall API
- Provides Magic: The Gathering card data
- No personal information shared
- Used only for card information lookup

## International Users

### Data Location
- Data primarily stored in Google Cloud servers
- May be processed in various countries where Google operates
- All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws

### GDPR Compliance (EU Users)
- Legal basis: Legitimate interest and contract performance
- Right to data portability, erasure, and rectification
- Data Protection Officer contact: privacy@magicai.app

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
